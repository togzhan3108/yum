Rice with gravy

Pour the washed rice with water, bring to a boil over high heat, salt, add turmeric, cumin and a few peas of allspice. Reduce the fire to a minimum and cook until the water is completely absorbed.

Grind chicken meat in a blender or meat grinder. Finely chop the onion. Grate carrots on a coarse grater. Fry the onions until lightly golden, add the carrots and fry until soft. Add minced meat, season with salt and stirring vigorously to break minced meat lumps, fry over high heat for five minutes. Add sour cream, paprika and thyme, simmer covered for 5-7 minutes. Put the finished rice on a plate, pour with hot gravy.
